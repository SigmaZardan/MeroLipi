Privacy Policy

MeroLipi - Nepali Keyboard

Effective Date: November 2, 2025

The MeroLipi keyboard does not collect any data. Everything you type using the keyboard stays completely private on your device. The transliteration feature (a separate tool within the app) uses Google Gemini API to convert Roman Nepali text to Devanagari.

Introduction

MeroLipi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Nepali keyboard application handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

Keyboard Usage

The MeroLipi keyboard does not collect any data. We do not:

  • Collect or access anything you type using the keyboard
  • Store your typing data or keyboard usage information
  • Track your location or device information through the keyboard
  • Transmit any keyboard data to our servers or third parties

Transliteration Feature

MeroLipi includes a separate transliteration feature within the app (not part of the keyboard). When you use this feature:

  • The text you enter in the transliteration view is sent to Google's Gemini API for conversion from Roman Nepali to Devanagari script
  • Only the text you explicitly type in the transliteration field is transmitted
  • The converted text is saved on your device for your convenience
  • This is an optional feature - you can use the keyboard without ever using transliteration

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Location data
  • Contact lists, photos, or any other personal content
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers

How the App Works

Keyboard

The MeroLipi keyboard operates entirely on your device. All keyboard text processing happens locally on your iPhone. The keyboard does not require an internet connection and no keyboard data ever leaves your device.

Transliteration Feature

The transliteration feature (separate from the keyboard) converts Roman Nepali text to Devanagari script using Google's Gemini API. This feature requires an internet connection and sends your input text to Google's servers for processing. The converted text is then returned and saved on your device.

Data Storage

Any preferences or saved phrases you create within MeroLipi are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Protected by your device's built-in security features
  • Permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Keyboard Permissions

iOS requires custom keyboards to request "Full Access" permission. While this permission is a system requirement, MeroLipi does not use it to collect any data. We maintain our strict no-data-collection policy regardless of the permissions granted.

Third-Party Services

MeroLipi uses the following third-party services:

Google Gemini API (Transliteration Feature Only)

When you use the transliteration feature within the app (not the keyboard), the text you enter in the transliteration view is sent to Google's Gemini API to convert Roman Nepali to Devanagari script. This feature:

  • Only applies to the dedicated transliteration view within the app
  • Does NOT affect the keyboard - nothing you type using the keyboard is sent anywhere
  • Requires an internet connection to function
  • Sends only the text you explicitly enter in the transliteration field to Google
  • The converted text is stored locally on your device using SwiftData

Google's processing of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Emoji Picker Library

MeroLipi uses an open-source emoji picker component that operates entirely on your device. No data is collected or transmitted by this component.

Important: MeroLipi does not use any analytics platforms or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

MeroLipi does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no user data that could be compromised. All data created by the app remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Your Privacy Rights

Because MeroLipi does not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete
  • You maintain complete control over any locally stored preferences
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling MeroLipi from your device
